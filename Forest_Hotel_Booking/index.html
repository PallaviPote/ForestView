About Us

We are a hotel property launched in July 2024 in the heart of Nanded City. We have standard and deluxe rooms for a comfortable and affordable price with a view of the Khadakwasla dam, the mighty Sahyadri range and the Sudan Block of NDA..Forest Retreat offers a peaceful escape into nature with comfortable rooms, tree houses, and luxury tents. Surrounded by lush greenery, we provide the perfect getaway for relaxation and adventure.
